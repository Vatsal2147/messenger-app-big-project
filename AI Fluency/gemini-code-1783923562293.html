Core Research Frontiers

Investigating critical interactions across the biosphere, lithosphere, and technosphere.

Air Quality & Climate Dynamics

Monitoring aerosol chemical composition, source apportionment modeling, and researching long-range atmospheric pollutant transport dynamics.

Labs: Aerosol Mass Spectrometry Lab, Climate Systems Modeling Center.

Water & Wastewater Engineering

Developing advanced membrane bioreactors, electrochemical remediation techniques, and tackling emerging contaminants like microplastics.

Labs: Hydro-Chemical Engineering Facility, Emerging Contaminants Research Unit.

Circular Economy & Clean Energy

Designing lifecycle assessment algorithms, thermo-chemical waste-to-energy conversion profiles, and smart microgrid urban layouts.

Labs: Industrial Ecology & LCA Studio, Bio-Energy Engineering Lab.
